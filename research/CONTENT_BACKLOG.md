## First complete path

Build one member journey all the way through before expanding broadly.

Suggested starting path:

1. New member joins a club
2. Selects goat and/or chicken projects
3. Finds official project requirements
4. Builds a backward calendar from fair week
5. Completes records and buyer outreach
6. Understands check-in, barn duty, showing, auction, and teardown
7. Records expenses, income, and lessons for next year
